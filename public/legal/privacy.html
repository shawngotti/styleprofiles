StyleProfiles — Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

StyleProfiles operates the StyleProfiles marketplace. Contact: Style Profiles, Louisville, KY — sgotti06@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account & profile: name, email, display name, role(s), city; for professionals, storefront details and geocoded location (latitude/longitude).
  • Bookings & households: appointments, household members you add (which may include minors you are responsible for), service history.
  • Payments: processed by Stripe; we store Stripe identifiers and money state (paid/refunded/subscription status), not full card numbers.
  • Media: portfolio, Awards, and The Lineup before/after images you upload or consent to appear in. Stored in private buckets, served via signed URLs.
  • Competition & engagement: votes, scores (judges), entries, event attendance, StylePoints ledger.
  • Analytics & device: usage events, profile-view records, approximate location if you use "near me," and basic device/log data. We use essential cookies and local storage to keep you signed in (Supabase Auth); Stripe and Sentry set limited cookies/identifiers to process payments and capture errors. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site ad tracking.

3. Sensitive & minors' data

  • Minors: we do not knowingly create accounts for under-18s. Adults may add minor household members and are responsible for that data and consent. Media depicting minors requires verifiable parental/guardian consent.
  • Media / biometric-adjacent: uploaded imagery (portfolio, reviews, competition entries) can show faces, hair, and skin in detail. We do not use facial recognition, and we do not create, derive, or store biometric identifiers or biometric information (such as faceprints or scans of face geometry) as those terms are defined under laws like the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA). Review text and images are sent to our moderation provider (see Section 5) solely to screen for spam and prohibited content.

4. How we use data

To provide bookings/payments/memberships; run loyalty, Awards, and The Lineup; detect vote/payment fraud; personalize discovery; communicate (notifications, transactional email); and meet legal obligations.

5. How we share data

  • Professionals you book or interact with (necessary booking/consent info).
  • Processors: Stripe (payments), Supabase (database, storage, authentication, hosting), Resend (transactional email), Sentry (error monitoring), OpenAI (automated screening of review text and uploaded images for spam and prohibited content), and Eventbrite (aggregating attendee records for tickets sold off-platform). Processors act on our instructions under their own terms.
  • Legal/safety as required by law or to protect users and the platform. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising, as those terms are defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA). Because we neither sell nor share, no "Do Not Sell or Share" opt-out is required; you may still exercise the rights in Section 8.

6. Consent & your media

Your Tag & Consent choices (public / anonymous / private / declined) control whether and how your likeness appears. You can change or withdraw consent for future use; previously published instances are removed on a best-effort, forward-looking basis.

7. Retention

We keep data while your account is active and as needed for legal, tax, fraud, and dispute purposes. As a general guide: account and profile data is kept for the life of your account; booking, payment, and StylePoints ledgers are retained for up to 7 years for tax, accounting, and dispute purposes; and moderation and security logs are kept for up to 2 years. We delete or de-identify data when it is no longer needed for these purposes.

8. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, and portability of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent, subject to applicable law and our legitimate retention needs. These rights are provided as required under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) and other applicable U.S. state privacy laws; if you are in a jurisdiction with additional rights (such as the EU/UK under the GDPR), those rights apply where the law requires. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights. Requests: sgotti06@gmail.com.

9. Security

RLS-enforced access control, private media buckets with signed URLs, server-authoritative money/points/votes, encryption in transit. No system is perfectly secure.

10. International / changes / contact

StyleProfiles is operated from the United States, and your information is stored and processed on infrastructure located in the United States. If you access StyleProfiles from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States. We post new versions (with a new version string) and re-prompt for acceptance where required. Contact: sgotti06@gmail.com.
