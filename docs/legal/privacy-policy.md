# StyleProfiles — Privacy Policy

## 1. Who we are
StyleProfiles operates the StyleProfiles marketplace. Contact: `[sgotti06@gmail.com]`.

## 2. Data we collect
- **Account & profile:** name, email, display name, role(s), city; for professionals, storefront details and geocoded location (latitude/longitude).
- **Bookings & households:** appointments, household members you add (which may include minors you are responsible for), service history.
- **Payments:** processed by **Stripe**; we store Stripe identifiers and money state (paid/refunded/subscription status), **not full card numbers**.
- **Media:** portfolio, Awards, and The Lineup before/after images you upload or consent to appear in. Stored in private buckets, served via signed URLs.
- **Competition & engagement:** votes, scores (judges), entries, event attendance, StylePoints ledger.
- **Analytics & device:** usage events, approximate location if you use "near me," and basic device/log data. `[counsel: confirm cookie/SDK disclosures]`.

## 3. Sensitive & minors' data
- **Minors:** we do not knowingly create accounts for under-18s. Adults may add minor household members and are responsible for that data and consent. Media depicting minors requires verifiable parental/guardian consent.
- **Media / biometric-adjacent:** uploaded imagery can show faces, hair, and skin in detail. We do **not** use facial recognition or derive biometric identifiers. `[counsel: BIPA/CCPA/“sensitive personal information” treatment and notices]`.

## 4. How we use data
To provide bookings/payments/memberships; run loyalty, Awards, and The Lineup; detect vote/payment fraud; personalize discovery; communicate (notifications, transactional email); and meet legal obligations.

## 5. How we share data
- **Professionals** you book or interact with (necessary booking/consent info).
- **Processors:** Stripe (payments), Supabase (database/storage/auth), `[email provider]`, `[analytics/error-monitoring provider]`, `[Eventbrite for ticket aggregation]`.
- **Legal/safety** as required. We do **not** sell personal information `[counsel: CCPA “sale/share” + opt-out]`.

## 6. Consent & your media
Your Tag & Consent choices (public / anonymous / private / declined) control whether and how your likeness appears. You can change or withdraw consent for future use; previously published instances are removed on a best-effort, forward-looking basis.

## 7. Retention
We keep data while your account is active and as needed for legal, tax, fraud, and dispute purposes. Ledgers (payments, points) are retained for record-keeping. `[counsel: retention schedule]`.

## 8. Your rights
Access, correction, deletion, portability, and consent withdrawal, subject to law and legitimate retention. `[counsel: CCPA/CPRA, GDPR if applicable, state privacy laws]`. Requests: `[sgotti06@gmail.com]`.

RLS-enforced access control, private media buckets with signed URLs, server-authoritative money/points/votes, encryption in transit. No system is perfectly secure.
